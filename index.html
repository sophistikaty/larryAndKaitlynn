Travel & Accomodations

Austin, Texas

Austin Nights from Andrew Takano on Vimeo.

We picked Austin, Texas as our destination for this wedding because we just love the city and everything it has to offer. It's such a refreshing place to visit and live in that it just exudes fun and energy from the moment you arrive. Even with the humidity in the summer no one can deny that Austin is the place to visit in Texas. Whether you like eating, drinking, dancing, hiking, swimming, or just relaxing on a field of grass, Austin has something for you.

We hope you enjoy the city while you're visiting, check out our going out guide for our suggestions and top picks of things to do!

Hotels

We plan to have two hotel room blocks reserved for ya'll to check out. We're going to pick out a high end downtown Austin hotel as well as one closer to the venue that is more reasonable in price. You are free to pick whatever you preferer. Hotel management let us know that we can reserve blocks beginning in about July, but you're welcome to make other arrangements if you plan ahead.

Remember that Austin City Limits (a huge music festival) is scheduled for the same weekend as our wedding and that means that hotel rooms are going to book really fast so if you want to ensure you get a good room at a good price you probably want to book early.

Also, here are a couple of great AirBnB options that we recommend in case you're looking for an alternative. Some of these we've stayed at and some just look really cool.

Austin Bergstrom International Airport (AUS)

The Austin Airport is one of the coolest airports we've ever been to. It doesn't miss a beat and makes sure that you know Austin is all about music, beer, and being weird. Trust us, you'll never be happier missing a flight than when you're at the Austin Airport. So make sure to grab some ice cream, a beer, and listen to a live band while you wait for your flight out.

The Airport is just southeast of Downtown Austin and north east of the wedding venue. If you're coming in on Saturday you'll want to account for a 40 minute drive.

Bear Creek Retreat

If you've never been to Texas before one thing we think you'll notice while you visit is the Texas open sky and fantastic outdoor afternoons as the sun sets and the cool breeze comes in. We wanted our wedding to capture Texas at its best, out in the open. Bear Creek allows us to do that and we're super excited to be at this venue.
